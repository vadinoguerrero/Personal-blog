Every once in a while my life gets those “good times” where everything feels downhill, problems stop hurting and what was once hard now feels easy.

I realize in those times I have the need to put into words what I was doing wrong, and how to not do it in the future, and my mind gives me the illusion that it’s all solved and I’m gonna stay that way forever.

But instead of naively hoping I’ll stay that way, it’s probably wiser to recognize beforehand that the same cycle of hardship -> learning -> fulfillment is gonna keep repeating over and over on multiple aspects of my life.

That’s what makes life interesting, that’s what keeps us moving and gives us purpose, otherwise there’d be no point to anything.
